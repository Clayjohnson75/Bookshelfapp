Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Introduction

Bookshelf Scanner ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Display name
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)

Photos and Images

With your permission, we access photos from your device to scan bookshelves. These images are:

  • Processed locally on your device when possible
  • Sent to our secure servers for AI-powered book detection
  • Not stored permanently on our servers after processing
  • Never shared with third parties except as necessary for book detection services

Book Data

Information about books you scan (titles, authors, etc.) is stored locally on your device and in your account for your personal use.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide book detection and cataloging services
  • To maintain your account and library
  • To improve our AI detection accuracy
  • To communicate with you about your account

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For user authentication and data storage
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered book detection
  • Google Gemini: For AI-powered book detection
  • Open Library API: For book cover images and metadata

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your personal information with these services beyond what is necessary to provide the app's functionality.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your account data is stored securely using Supabase
  • Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Photos are processed and not permanently stored on our servers
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Opt out of data collection (note: this may limit app functionality)

Camera and Photo Library Access

The app requires access to your camera and photo library to scan bookshelves. This permission:

  • Is only used when you explicitly take a photo or select an image
  • Is not used to access other photos on your device
  • Can be revoked at any time through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
