Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan my bookshelf?

Open the app, tap the camera button, and take a photo of your bookshelf. The AI will automatically detect all visible books.

Can I upload existing photos?

Yes! Tap the photo library button to select an existing image from your device.

How accurate is the book detection?

Our AI uses advanced technology to detect books with high accuracy. For best results, ensure books are clearly visible and well-lit.

Where is my data stored?

Your book data is stored securely in your account and can be accessed from any device where you're signed in.

Can I edit detected books?

Yes! Tap on any book in your library to edit the title, author, or other information.
